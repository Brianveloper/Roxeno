Our Story

Roxeno Enterprises is a leading general supplies company based in Kenya. With a commitment to excellence, we provide a wide range of products including foodstuffs, non-foodstuff, washing materials, cutlery, stationery, and more. Our mission is to meet the diverse needs of our clients by delivering top-quality supplies.

We take pride in serving some of Kenya's top institutions, ensuring that they receive reliable and cost-effective solutions for their everyday requirements. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to maintain the highest standards of service and reliability.
